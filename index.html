The problem

Lab storage is broken

In shared research labs, samples go missing, get mislabelled, or end up at the wrong temperature. Hours are wasted, experiments fail, and expensive reagents get reordered unnecessarily.

Hours lost per week

Researchers waste time searching instead of doing science — samples hidden in the wrong fridge or mislabelled box.

Compromised samples

Wrong temperatures ruin samples silently. No automated monitoring means damage goes undetected until experiments fail.

No access control

Sensitive samples are accessible to everyone, leading to accidental contamination, mix-ups, and "sample theft".

The solution

How ASSS works

For lab researchers who want to easily locate their samples, ASSS is a smart storage system that provides optimised sample organisation — with automatic reordering and pre-dilution of stocks.
1

Log in with your researcher ID

Scan your card for role-based access. Only see and retrieve samples you're authorised to use.

2

Search and select your sample

Find any sample by name, ID, or category on the built-in screen. Results appear instantly.

3

The machine retrieves it for you

The correct temperature zone is selected automatically. Your sample is dispensed within 30 seconds.

4

Return, log, reorder — automatically

On return, the system re-sorts the sample, logs usage for reproducibility, and auto-reorders when stock is low.
